What kinds of information do we collect?

We collect the content, communications and other information you provide when you use our Product, including when you sign up for an account, create or share content, and message or communicate with others. This can include information in or about the content you provide (like metadata), such as the location of a photo or the date a file was created. It can also include what you see through features we provide, such as our camera to get the information.

We collect information about the people, Pages, accounts, hashtags, and groups you are connected to and how you interact with them in our Product, such as people you communicate with the most or groups you are part of.

We take permissions like record audio,record video,Location,contacts, Files Access for users better usage of the platform we won't share data with other third parties.

User Details: when you do Sign up, you are required to give information like Email Id, Phone number. You can also save your Personal information like DOB, Address, and Social Media ids in your profile.
